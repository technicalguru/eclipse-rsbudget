History Settings

This tab defines what information RsBudget will display in the History View of RsBudget.

By default, all active accounts (see Account Settings) will be displayed along with their monthly status change. However, you can define additional values to be recorded and displayed, e.g. the status of your car mileage display or electrical power meter.

Profit/Loss Threshold

This field defines an additional threshold for your monthly planning. Enter an amount that you consider to be the minimum to be left when a month is closed. E.g. enter 1,000.00 USD when you want at least this amount to be available at your accounts. RsBudget will calculate and display a relative balance within the Monthly Planning View so you can control this figure.

Additional Historical Values

The table contains all those values to be displayed in addition to your active accounts (see Account Settings). Right-click on this table to create, delete or move a value up or down.

You are able to modify a value in the table by double-clicking on it. Name will be the label of your additional value, Unit will hold the unit of the value, e.g. km, USD, EUR. By selecting Display Change, RsBudget will add an additional column in the History View displaying the difference of the value to the previous month. Finally, Format controls whether fractions will be displayed or not.
