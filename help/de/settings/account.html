Account Settings

This tab lists the bank accounts for your RsBudget installation. You can create, modify and change accounts.

Accounts currently have no further meaning. However, each transaction record needs to be attached to a bank account to enable future enhancements, e.g. HBCI downloads from your bank.

Create a new Account

Click the "Create Account" link. This will create and select a new record. Please refer to the Change Account section to fill the form.

Change Account

Following fields can be modified:

Field Description
Name Each account is referred to by its name. Please choose a meaningful name.
Owner The owner of the account. This is usually your name.
Acct. Number The number of your account.
BIC Bank Identification Code. Select the bank. If your bank is not listed (German banks only), please choose any bank.
Default This flag marks the account as the default account. RsBudget will automatically attach all transactions with this account. However, you can select the account for each individual transaction.
Planning Relevant The account will be included in the plan calculations. Remove the flag when this account shall be used for statistical or informational reasons only (e.g. for Share Depots).

Delete an Account

Click the "Delete Account" link. RsBudget will ask you for confirmation and then remove the account from the list. However, the account is not physically deleted but marked as inactive only. You can revert your action when you cancel the Preferences dialog. Once you confirmed all changes with "OK", the deactivation will be performed and there is currently no possibility to re-enable the account (Future Enhancement).
